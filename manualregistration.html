Manual Registration

Don't get the registration to work? No worries, we can help you make a manual registration!

The regular registration may not work because you're not in the database yet.

Please fill out the manual registration form and we will manually validate your information. You'll later get an email when the registration is done, where you'll be able to login with username and password.

But what do we really do? We'll check that the information is correct and add you to the database. If the information is incorrect, we'll get back to you.
